About-Us

UniBite.

A story of hungry frustrated students

OUR STORY

"It all started with a shared frustration: long waiting lines to order food. We saw students and staff wasting valuable time just to grab a meal. Inspired to make a difference, we created UniBite!"

OUR MISSION

"We envisioned a platform that eliminates the need to wait altogether—something simple, fast, and convenient. With UniBite, browse menus, order your food online, and get notified the moment your meal is ready!"

OUR VISION

"We aim to redefine campus dining by making food access smarter, faster, and more personal. Our vision is to expand UniBite into a seamless ecosystem where students can discover, customize, and enjoy meals—without the hassle!"
